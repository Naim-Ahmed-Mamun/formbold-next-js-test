### General Settings

**Form Name:**
The name of form you created to identify form. Name won't be shown to your visitors.

**Form Enabled:**
Enabled by default, you can choose whether to allow new submissions or not.

**Submission Archive:**
Choose whether to store submissions data and attachments in FormBold. If disabled, data and file uploads won't stored.

### Submission Processing

**Email Notifications:**
Enable or disable sending notification emails of all submissions. Submissions will still be updated to the archive and dispatched to apps (if connected).

**Target Email:**
You can choose target email to send submissions of specific form. to add a new email address, please visit the [account page](https://formbold.com/account)

**Redirect URL:**
You can specify URL to redirect after successful submission [PRO]

**HTTP API:**
Enable this for allowing programmatic access to form submissions [PRO]
