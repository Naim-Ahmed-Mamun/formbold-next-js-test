## Fillout Overview

Fillout is designed for individuals and businesses wanting **unlimited forms and users** on a free plan, plus powerful features like multipage forms, PDF generation, and scheduling. It emphasizes workflow automation and form analytics, making it ideal for scaling data intake and business processes.

- Unlimited users and forms even on free plan
- Supports advanced conditional logic and workflows
- Custom domains and analytics on higher tiers
- Suitable for businesses needing detailed data handling and reporting

## Why Choose FormBold Over Fillout

Fillout is great for complex workflows and scheduling. But if you're a startup, developer, or small team looking for simple pricing, white-labeling, and easy integration—FormBold wins.

**Best Use Cases:**

- Budget-friendly for devs/startups — FormBold
- Unlimited basic forms — Fillout
- Complex logic & automation — Fillout
- Scheduling and PDF output — Fillout
- Small teams with Slack/Discord needs — FormBold

FormBold offers more freedom, better pricing, and faster setup—perfect for lean teams and real projects. Start free. Pay only when you grow.

## Final Verdict – Fillout vs FormBold

Choose Fillout if you need advanced features like scheduling, PDF generation, and analytics for business workflows. **Choose FormBold** if you want a simple, developer-friendly tool with easy setup, unlimited forms, and white-labeling
at a lower cost—perfect for startups and agencies.

## FAQ Section

1. **Can I create unlimited forms on the free plan?**

	Fillout allows unlimited forms and seats with 1,000 responses/month, while FormBold offers 5 forms with 100 submissions/month.

2. **Does Fillout support file uploads?**

	Yes, unlimited file uploads are available on all Fillout plans.

3. **Can I remove form branding on the free plan?**

	Fillout requires a paid plan to remove branding, but FormBold includes branding removal even on the free plan for forms.

4. **Which tool offers workflow automation?**

	Fillout includes a dedicated Workflows feature. FormBold supports basic automation via webhooks and integrations.

5. **What integrations are available?**

	Fillout supports Airtable, Google Sheets, Notion, and ,more tools. FormBold integrates with Slack, Discord, Notion, and offers API/webhook support.
