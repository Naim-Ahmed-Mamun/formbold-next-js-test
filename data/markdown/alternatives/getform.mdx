## Getform Overview
Getform is a backend platform built for handling static form submissions, especially useful for Jamstack or static websites. It provides solid core features—but only behind its paid tiers. The **lowest plan starts at $15.83/month**, and essential features like autoresponders, branding removal, or email templates are only unlocked at **$40.
83/month or higher**.

While Getform supports file uploads and has polished UI features, its pricing makes it **less appealing for solo developers or small teams** looking for speed, simplicity, and flexibility.

## Why Choose FormBold Over Getform?
FormBold is built to **remove roadblocks** for developers. No branding, no paywall for essentials, and no lock-in to expensive plans.

- Only $4/month (yearly plan) to unlock unlimited forms and key features. Getform charges 4–10x more for the same.
- Free plan includes 100 submissions and 5 forms
- No credit card or yearly billing required to start
- Developer-friendly with REST API and JSON responses

## Final Verdict - Getform or FormBold?
FormBold is best for budget-friendly forms, static sites, autoresponders, and full branding control. Getform is better for large file uploads and advanced email setups but at higher costs.

So, If you're a developer or team looking for clean, powerful, and affordable form management—FormBold beats Getform on price, flexibility, and developer tools.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. **Is FormBold better than Web3Forms for integrations?**

	Yes. FormBold offers built-in integration with Slack, Notion, Google Sheets, Discord, Telegram, and more—even on free plans.

2. **Does Web3Forms provide a submission dashboard?**

	No. Web3Forms does not have a dashboard to manage or export submissions. You rely on email notifications or third-party tools.

3. **Can I use FormBold with platforms like Webflow or Framer?**

	Yes, FormBold works seamlessly with modern no-code tools like Webflow, Framer, and static site builders.

4. **Which service is more affordable in the long term?**

	FormBold starts at just $4/month with flexible montly billing. Web3Forms requires a yearly commitment starting at $149/year ($12.4/month), which may not suit all developers.

5. **What's the biggest benefit of FormBold?**

	FormBold gives you advanced features—like analytics, file uploads, CSV export, and third-party integrations—without forcing you into high-tier plans.
