## Formcarry Overview
**Formcarry** is a straightforward form backend designed for developers and small businesses looking for reliability and scaling options. It offers white-label features and integration flexibility.

- Good for small businesses and teams
- Robust integration limits and team management
- Higher pricing for premium plans but strong file storage
- Simple form limits on lower tiers

## Why Choose FormBold Over Formcarry

FormBold offers simple, affordable pricing starting at $4/month with unlimited forms and APIs. It includes branding removal, custom domains, and integrations like Slack and Notion on all plans. Designed for developers, it works well for portfolios, SaaS, and more.

- Built-in Dashboard to view, filter, and export submissions
- Free Plan includes autoresponders, CSV export, and webhooks
- Developer Tools like real-time analytics
- Native Integrations with Slack, Discord, Telegram, Notion, and Google Sheets
- Starts at $4/month with flexible upgrade options
- Spam Protection with filters, domain lock, and reCAPTCHA

## Final Verdict – Formcarry vs FormBold
Formcarry is a good choice if you need large file uploads, more integrations, and support for bigger teams, but it costs more as you grow. FormBold is better if you want an easy, developer-friendly, and affordable option with 500 forms, flexible integrations, and branding control even on the free plan.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. **Does Formcarry allow unlimited forms on free plans?**

	No, only 1 form on the free plan. Unlimited forms start from paid plans. Whereas Formbold offers 5 forms even on their free plan.

2. **Can I upload files with FormBold?**

	Yes, file upload support is included starting with any pro plans.

3. **Which supports more team members?**

	Formcarry supports up to 20 team members on premium plans; FormBold also supports teams but with more flexible pricing.

4. **Who is FormBold best for?**

	Developers, indie makers, and teams that value simplicity, fast setup, and flexibility over bloated enterprise dashboards.

5. **What integrations do they support?**

	FormBold includes Slack, Discord, Notion, and 5+ more integrations. Formcarry offers built-in integrations and API calls.
